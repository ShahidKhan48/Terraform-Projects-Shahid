## üîç Key Topics Covered

### **Evolution Timeline**
1. **Manual Infrastructure** (1990s-2000s)
2. **Configuration Management** (2000s-2010s)
3. **Cloud Computing** (2010s)
4. **Infrastructure as Code** (2010s-present)
5. **GitOps & Policy as Code** (present-future)

### **Tool Ecosystem**
- **Declarative Tools**: Terraform, CloudFormation, ARM Templates
- **Imperative Tools**: Ansible, Chef, Puppet, SaltStack
- **Hybrid Approaches**: Pulumi, CDK
- **Cloud-Native**: Kubernetes YAML, Helm Charts

### **Modern Practices**
- GitOps workflows
- Policy as Code (OPA, Sentinel)
- Infrastructure testing
- Compliance automation
- Cost optimization

## üí° Interview Tips

### **What Interviewers Look For**
- Understanding of infrastructure evolution
- Practical experience with IaC tools
- Knowledge of best practices and patterns
- Ability to explain complex concepts simply
- Real-world problem-solving experience

### **Common Follow-up Questions**
- "How would you migrate from manual to IaC?"
- "What challenges have you faced with IaC adoption?"
- "How do you handle infrastructure drift?"
- "What's your approach to testing infrastructure?"

### **Red Flags to Avoid**
- Only theoretical knowledge without practical experience
- Inability to explain trade-offs between tools
- Not understanding the business value of IaC
- Lack of knowledge about modern practices
